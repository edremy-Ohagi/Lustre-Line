# Founder Feedback Checklist

Use this after sharing the first MVP preview with the founder.

## Visual Direction

- Does the page feel like `轻奢温柔`?
- Should the style lean more modern luxury or more subtle oriental elegance?
- Does the logo feel suitable in the header?
- Is the first screen attractive enough for mobile visitors?

## Product Display

- Are the expanded categories complete, especially 热门款式、手串、手镯、标本、高端珠宝 and 情侣款式?
- Which of the six current low-resolution product photos should receive a high-resolution replacement first?
- Do the catalogue product names and price ranges match the current in-store offering?
- Does keyword search return the expected products for materials and gemstones?
- Should product cards show more or less information?
- Are `新品 / 推荐 / 定制` the right first labels?

## Product Details

- Are material, gemstone, size, scene, and description enough for the first version?
- Should every product have a dedicated detail page?
- Does the modal feel convenient on mobile?

## Price And Conversion

- Which products should show fixed prices?
- Which products should show `询问价格`?
- Is `立即咨询` the right primary CTA?
- Should Xiaohongshu or WeChat be emphasized more?

## Contact

- Confirm that the integrated WeChat QR code and WeChat ID `sept` are correct.
- Provide the real Xiaohongshu QR code or profile link.
- Confirm whether a phone number, email, or store address should appear.
- Confirm whether a contact form is needed later.

## Next Phase

- Decide whether to replace placeholder content before backend planning.
- Decide whether to freeze the frontend MVP scope.
- Confirm when to start admin/backend planning.
